Our Story

Shrigonda's Favorite Since 1996

Trimurti Bhel Centre was founded in 1996 by Mr. Rajendra Pawar with a small cart in Kumbhar Galli. What started as a humble venture to support his family soon became Shrigonda's most loved street food destination.

The name "Trimurti" represents the three essential elements of our food - taste, quality, and hygiene. These principles have guided us for over 25 years.

Today, run by Rajendra's sons, we continue the legacy using the same traditional recipes and techniques that made us famous, while adapting to modern standards.

Our Journey

1996 - The Beginning

Started as a small cart with just 3 items: Bhel, Sev Puri and Vada Pav. Quickly gained popularity for authentic taste.

2002 - First Shop

Moved to a permanent location in Kumbhar Galli, expanding the menu to include Dabeli, Misal and other snacks.

2010 - Recognition

Featured in local newspapers as one of Shrigonda's must-try street foods. Started catering for local events.

2020 - Next Generation

Rajendra's sons took over operations, introducing digital payments and modern hygiene practices while preserving traditional recipes.
